Controls

Behavior summary:

  • Power: Short press toggles Off ↔ On. On power-up, LED fast-blinks in preferred color for 10 blinks, then goes solid Green (run).
  • Menu entry (Run only): Hold ≥ 5s. LED slow-blinks candidate color; short-press cycles Red → Green → Yellow → Red.
  • Confirm selection: Long-press (≥ 1s) → LED fast-blinks 10 times in chosen color, then exit menu to solid Green. Preferred color is updated after those 10 blinks complete.
  • Preview (Run): Short-press Menu to check preferred color (2 slow blinks), then return to solid Green.
  • Power during menu: powers Off immediately, aborts menu/confirm, and does not change the preferred color.
  • Persistence: Preferred color persists across reloads (localStorage). Default on first run: Red.

Event Log

Blink rates: fast ≈ 6 Hz (period ≈ 166.7 ms), slow ≈ 1.5 Hz (period ≈ 666.7 ms). Timing uses setInterval; slight jitter is expected.
